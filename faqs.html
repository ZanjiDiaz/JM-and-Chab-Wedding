Frequently Asked questions

What time should I be there?

The ceremony will start at exactly 3:00 PM. We ask you to be there 1 hour before the exact time to avoid delays.

A gentle reminder:
Don't starve yourself. Please eat your lunch before going to the venue.

Are my kids welcome?

While we love your little ones, we wanted you to relax and enjoy the day. We appreciate you for making arrangements ahead of time and leaving kids at home so you can celebrate with us.

We truly wish we could invite all of our guests' children to our wedding; however, we are only able to invite the children of our immediate families.

Can i bring a plus one?

Unfortunately, due to budget restriction, we carefully chose our wedding guests. We don't want unexpected circumstances that will put us into financial distress after the wedding. We only allotted our budget to the people we invited.

Aside from that, we are at capacity with our venue which means we are sadly unable to accommodate additional guests. We look forward to celebrating with you on the day!

How do I reserve a seat?

Please fill-out this RSVP form-> RSVP Form

What will happen if I don't fill out the RSVP? Is it really important?

It is important for us to determine the number of guests coming. If you do not fill-out RSVP on time, we will understand that you are not available to celebrate with us.

What if I still go even if I did not fill-out the RSVP?

Since your name is not listed, unfortunately, there will be no seat and no food available for you. We don't want that to happen.

Is there a dress code?

We'd love to see our family and friends dress up with us!

No jeans, t-shirt or sneakers, please!

Please refer to this page for the dresscode -> Dresscode

Hopefully, our wedding website FAQS will clear up all of essential details you should know, but just in case you have more questions, kindly message us directly.
